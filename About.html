Our Firm

Founded in London in 2008, Richards Architecture strives to impact people through places. No matter what project we’re designing, we always aim to make a difference in the city’s landscape. We envision spaces that will inspire people, help them connect with one another, and enrich the human experience. We do this by embracing the life, love, and diversity of the city. Recognizing that every great design is the result of a combination of ideas, we’re inspired by the dynamism of urban life. You’ll be surprised by what we can do for you. We’d love to work with you on your next project.
