Town Housing Project

Welcome to our news page!
Here you can find our recent projects and business updates, including our current major development project of the Bournemouth Housing Development Scheme.

The Project Brief and Client

For this project, we were approached as a small architects firm by the Bournemouth Borough Council (Now known as 'Dorset Council'), to design and develop a conceptual housing scheme to house several different economically stable families.
Some examples of these families include first time buyers, couples with one-two children, families with a stable income, middle-aged families and lastly, families reaching retirement age.
In response to the rising environmental climate change, we included several features including natural lighting, solar panels, eco-friendly insulation, biodegradable materials, and so much more!
We worked tirelessly to produce several different models, both virtual and physical, using emerging digital technologies such as Virtual Reality (VR) to create a 3D digital mock-up of the housing so that the client would be able to gain a deeper understanding of how the properties would look and feel for the home owners.

Due to the complexity and client confidentiality, we are currently unavailable to reveal more information on this project, as it is currently ongoing. However, please visit our Social Media sites for more information on our previous projects, or feel free to get in touch!
