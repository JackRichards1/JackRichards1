Town Housing Project

Welcome to our news page!
Here you can find our recent projects and business updates, including our current major development project of the Bournemouth Housing Development Scheme.

The Project Brief and Client

For this project, we were approached as a small architects firm by the Bournemouth Borough Council (Now known as 'Dorset Council'), to design and develop a conceptual housing scheme to house several different economically stable families.
Some examples of these families include first time buyers, couples with one-two children, families with a stable income, middle-aged families and lastly, families reaching retirement age.
